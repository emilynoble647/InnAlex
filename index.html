About this space

This meticulously designed 3 b/room NEW Home Unit is perfect for families/couples & pets. It's only 5 min walk to Alex H/land Beach, cafes, restaurants & shops. The location is what people love! North facing with glimpses of the surf, this beautiful Unit has all the comforts of home to relax and unwind. It has a well equipped kitchen & laundry, secure private front yard for kids and pets, alfresco dining, lap pool & onsite secure parking. You and your family will have the best holiday ever! The space You will love this Impressive Home Unit that has just been completed by the owner's Neil and Sherie. It replaces their old beach house, which was one of the early Airbnbs here in Alexandra Headland. Its great location is only a short walk to the stunning patrolled beaches of Alexandra Headland, Alex Surf Club and several Cafes and restaurants and only 3 minutes walk to the sand! The holiday Unit is on the first floor of our beautiful new building. It has a large well equipped kitchen with stone bench tops, dishwasher, oven, microwave and cooktop. We have a Nespresso Coffee machine and an array of coffee pods for your use, plus a selection of Tea if you prefer. We can also include a thermomix if you’d like. (With a quick demo first) The open plan living room features a comfortable Freedom modular sofa and a good size smart TV with Netflix for watching those fav. movies or series whilst you are holidays. Bedrooms 1 and 2 have queen size ensemble beds with built-in robes and bed-side tables. Bedroom 3 has a king single bed plus a 'trundle bunk bed' with double below and single above, allowing four to sleep here. it has a built-in robe plus a port shelf. All 3 bedrooms are air-conditioned and also have ceiling fans. Cosy fresh crisp linen is provided. There are 2 fully tiled bathrooms. The Main Bathroom has a fully equipped Laundry attached. Next to this is a separate Powder Room with toilet and vanity basin. Your second Bathroom is in the basement. Access to this is via the lift or internal foyer stairs. It has shower toilet and basin all in one. The unit also boasts a terrace with a servery outside the kitchen window and a Webber BBQ next to this, allowing user-friendly indoor and outdoor cooking. The outdoor dining table is here on the Terrace and can sit up to eight. This overlooks the secure Private Court Yard which is perfect for young children and your small dog. It is a great area to relax and unwind after a day exploring the Sunshine Coast. After spending a few years travelling the world, we have come to realise that the Sunshine Coast has one of the best climates in the world and Alexandra Headland has its own micro-climate. Our hot days usually have northerly winds. Here on Alex's North facing Headland, these northerly winds blow straight off the ocean and keep our temperatures down. When the strong south easterlies are blowing we are protected from these by Mooloolaba and our Headland and we just get the lower temperatures. With our sub tropical climate it is hard to beat for good weather.
